Sub Bass

20-60 Hz
The lowest frequencies we can hear and feel
Kick drum fundamental, bass guitar lowest notes, synth bass

Bass

60-250 Hz
Warmth and body of most instruments
Kick drum body, bass guitar main tone, low toms, male vocal chest

Low Mids

250-500 Hz
Fullness and body
Snare body, guitar body, vocal warmth

Mids

500 Hz - 2 kHz
Presence and definition of most instruments
Vocal clarity, guitar presence, snare crack

Upper Mids

2-4 kHz
Attack, clarity, and intelligibility
Vocal consonants, cymbal definition, string attack

Presence

4-6 kHz
Clarity and "forward" sound
Vocal intelligibility, cymbal shimmer

Brilliance

6-20 kHz
Air, sparkle, and shimmer
Cymbals, acoustic guitar shimmer, vocal breathiness
